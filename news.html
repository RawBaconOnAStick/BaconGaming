A New Game is Coming.

May 29th

Bacon Gaming Studio is proud to announce the development of the latest game to its collection, Forever Fight: 2019. Forever Fight: 2019 is the first of many from the X Fight series. The X fight series are a set of fighting games that all center around the same concept of player vs player. Each game features a different type of character, for example, Superheroes, Aliens, Gladiators, Vikings, etc. Though BG Studio is releasing the original, Forever Fight, they have not made any statements regarding the making of sequels. Rohan Dharwadker thinks that their time could be better spent making differen types of games first. No release date is officially set, but Forever Fight is expected to release around late June.
